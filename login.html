Should I Quit My Job to Pursue My Startup Idea?

This is an issue that a lot of future entrepreneurs grapple with. It’s a tough one, and a deeply personal one. Without knowing details about your business, your job, your personal life and your finances, I can’t really give you an answer. But in every in-depth conversation I’ve had with people considering quitting their job, the wisest decision is “no” as often as it’s “yes.” And if you’re just now beginning to consider the prospect, it may be too early.
